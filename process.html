The Process

A clear path from first conversation to final walkthrough.

Every project follows a deliberate rhythm. Nothing rushed, nothing skipped, nothing a surprise. Here's what each phase actually looks like.

01

Space Planning & Layout

Before a single finish or piece of furniture is discussed, we figure out how the room should actually function. How you move through it. Where you sit in the morning, where people land when they walk in, what needs to breathe, what can be tightened.

Good design starts with the floor plan. Always.

02

Concept & Mood Development

A clear creative direction for the space — materials, palette, light, texture, atmosphere. Pulled together as a visual reference you can see, react to, and push back on before a single decision gets made.

The goal is for you to feel the room before it's built.

03

Materials & Finish Selection

Paint, tile, flooring, fixtures, hardware. Every surface, considered — because the finishes are what you'll touch, clean, and live with every day.

I bring samples, narrow the options, and walk through the trade-offs so you're making informed decisions, not overwhelmed ones.

04

Furniture Sourcing & Styling

Finding the right pieces, at the right scale, for the right price point. Curated selections based on your space and budget — not a mood board, a shortlist built to be approved.

Trade-vendor access means pricing you wouldn't find on your own, and 5% of that savings comes back to you.

05

Vendor & Contractor Coordination

I work alongside your build team — contractors, tradespeople, installers — so the design translates correctly from paper to reality.

That means being the point of contact for design decisions during the build, catching issues before they become problems, and making sure the intent of the design survives the execution.

06

Full Project Management

Keeping everything on track from kickoff to the final walkthrough. Budgets watched, timelines held, orders chased, questions answered — so you can focus on your life while the project moves.

07

Live Project Documentation

Every active project gets a shared working document — pricing, links, notes, measurements, order status, and updates — all in one place. No digging through email threads, no wondering what was decided two weeks ago.

You always know where the project stands.

08

Final Styling & Walkthrough

The last layer — the details only you'll notice. Books on the shelves, throws where they belong, art hung at the right height, the last decisions made in person once everything else is in place.

Then a walkthrough together, so you see the space the way it was meant to be seen the first time.
