Services

Three ways to work together.

Whether you're starting from scratch, refreshing a single room, or just need direction — there's a scope that fits.

01 / Full Service

Full-Service Design

Top to bottom — from the initial concept all the way through to final styling. For clients starting from scratch, a complete renovation, or a room that needs to be rebuilt from the ground up.

  • Space planning & layout
  • Concept & mood development
  • Materials & finish selection — paint, tile, flooring, fixtures, hardware
  • Furniture sourcing & styling at the right scale and price point
  • Vendor & contractor coordination with your build team
  • Full project management — kickoff to final walkthrough
  • Live shared project document with pricing, links, and order updates
  • Final styling & the details only you'll notice
Best For

New builds, renovations, full-room redesigns

Timeline

Varies by project scope

02 / Consultations

Design Consultations

A focused session — in person on LBI or virtual — to work through a specific question, a stuck room, or a decision that needs an expert eye. Direction without the full scope.

  • In-home or virtual consultation
  • Layout & furniture placement feedback
  • Paint & finish recommendations
  • Lighting & styling direction
  • Honest assessment of what's working, what isn't
  • Written follow-up with notes & suggestions
Best For

One room, one decision, one question

Format

In-person on LBI or virtual

03 / Sourcing

Product Sourcing

Furniture, lighting, textiles, and finishes — curated at the right scale, for the right price point, with trade-access pricing passed through to you. Delivered as a clear approval document so you see every option before anything is ordered.

  • Curated product selections based on your space & scale
  • Trade pricing at participating vendors & showrooms
  • 5% discount on trade purchases passed through to clients
  • Pricing, links, notes & measurements documented in one place
  • Approval-based — nothing ordered without your sign-off
  • Order tracking & delivery coordination
Best For

Clients with design direction who need the right pieces

Deliverable

Shared project document + pricing

Why the 5%?

Trade-vendor access, passed to you.

As a trade-account holder, I'm responsible for remitting sales tax on all purchases each quarter — which comes out of the discount on my end. Sharing 5% rather than the full trade amount keeps things fair and transparent for both of us. A current list of participating vendors is available on request.

Not sure which fits?

Tell me a little about the project and the space — I'll help you figure out whether it's a full-service build, a single consultation, or something in between.
