About

Hi, I'm Jordan.

A solo interior designer based on Long Beach Island, New Jersey — designing minimal, calm, intentional coastal homes for the people who live in them.

The Story

Weekend trips to HomeGoods, eventually a career.

"Growing up, weekend trips to HomeGoods with my mom and flipping through Better Homes & Gardens were just things I enjoyed."

It wasn't until later that I realized those small habits were pointing toward something. The older I got, the more interiors pulled me in — and eventually I stopped ignoring it.

Now it's what I do full time. Jordan Lynn Interiors is a solo studio based on Long Beach Island, with a focus on beach and coastal homes. My style tends toward minimalism — spaces that feel intentional and calm without being cold. But above all, I design around the person living there, not the other way around.

The standard.

Home isn't just a place you return to at the end of the day. It's the backdrop of your life. It's what you look at over your morning coffee, what surrounds you when you unwind, what quietly reflects who you are to everyone who walks through the door.

It should exude you — not just on the surface, but in the details that only you'll notice. That's the standard I hold every project to. And it's the experience every client deserves.

How I work.

My role is part designer, part translator. I listen, I narrow down, I guide — and I bring the creative instinct to turn what you're feeling into something you can actually live inside. Every decision is intentional, every finish chosen with purpose, every detail considered so you don't have to think twice about it.

I'm a licensed interior designer with trade access at a wide range of vendors and showrooms. I work alongside your build team, keep everything documented in one shared project file, and manage the process from kickoff to final walkthrough.
