The Approach

"I don't design around my taste. I design around yours."

Not a version of you curated from a Pinterest board or a showroom floor — but the real you. The one with opinions, with taste, with a vision that maybe you haven't quite found the words for yet. About Jordan

Weekend trips to HomeGoods, eventually a career.

Growing up, weekend trips to HomeGoods with my mom and flipping through Better Homes & Gardens were just things I enjoyed. It wasn't until later that I realized those small habits were pointing toward something.

The older I got, the more interiors pulled me in — and eventually I stopped ignoring it. Now it's what I do full time. Jordan Lynn Interiors is a solo studio based on Long Beach Island, with a focus on beach and coastal homes.

My style tends toward minimalism — intentional and calm without being cold. But above all, I design around the person living there, not the other way around.

— Jordan
